<h1 class="title" style='font-family: "Linden Hill", Palatino, serif;'>Tirui (Steve) Cao</h1>

<h2 style='font-family: "Linden Hill", Palatino, serif;'><em>Final-Year PhD Student, Tsinghua University</em></h2>


<div class="intro">

Hi there, I'm Tirui Cao, and some friends call me Steve.
I am currently a final-year PhD student in the [Department of Industrial Engineering](https://www.ie.tsinghua.edu.cn/eng/) at [Tsinghua University](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/), advised by Professor [Lei Zhao](https://www.ie.tsinghua.edu.cn/eng/info/1051/1016.htm).
During my PhD, I visited the [MIT Sloan School of Management](https://mitsloan.mit.edu/) as a visiting student, hosted by Professor [Alexandre Jacquillat](https://mitsloan.mit.edu/faculty/directory/alexandre-jacquillat).
<!-- This visit was financially supported by THU Doctoral Grant \& TSL Cross-Regional Doctoral Grant. -->

Broadly speaking, I develop mathematical models and scalable algorithms for large-scale combinatorial optimization problems, with a focus on transportation science and logistics ([TSL](https://connect.informs.org/tsl/home)).
My research is motivated by emerging applications and practical challenges faced by leading industry companies such as [JD Logistics](https://www.jingdonglogistics.com/), [SF Express](https://www.sf-international.com/us/en/), and [Alibaba Group](https://www.alibaba.com/).
Methodologically, I focus in particular on exact methods---such as _Dantzig–Wolfe decomposition_, _column generation_, and _branch-price-and-cut_---for integer optimization, as well as on _approximate dynamic programming_ (now more widely known as _reinforcement learning_) for sequential decision-making under uncertainty.

I am also strongly interested in applying my expertise in optimization and analytics to _real-world_ problems.
I have participated in industry collaboration projects with [Huawei](https://www.huawei.com/en/), [COSCO Shipping](https://lines.coscoshipping.com/home/), and [OOCL](https://www.oocl.com/schi/Pages/default.aspx).
Additionally, I interned at [Alibaba Group](https://www.alibaba.com/) and [Meituan](https://www.meituan.com/en-US/about-us), which broadened my methodological toolkit to include _causal inference_ and _machine learning_.


</div>
