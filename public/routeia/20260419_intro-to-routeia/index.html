Introduction to Routeia

Steve 2026-04-19

Routeia (/ˈraʊtiə/) is my small, evolving research initiative for applying optimization and analytics to interesting decision problems, starting with transportation science and logistics (TSL) and extending beyond it. Through Routeia, I aim to pursue practice-driven, openly shared research that can inspire both researchers and practitioners. To start, I plan to publish technical notes and blog posts on my personal website.
